Monkeys!

Monkeys are one of the closest relatives to humans. They are incredibly intelligent and have opposable thumbs. There are more than 260 different types of monkeys and they are separated into two major categories: New World and Old World. New World monkeys live in the Americas and the Old World monkeys live in Asia and Africa.
