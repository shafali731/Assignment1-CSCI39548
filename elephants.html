Elephants!

Elephants are the largest mammals on earth. There are three living species: the African bush elephant, the African forest elephant, and the Asian elephant. They are scattered throughout sub-Saharan Africa, South Asia, and Southeast Asia all in different habitatis, incluing savannahs, forests, deserts, and marshes. Elephants are herbivores and stay near water when it is accessible.
